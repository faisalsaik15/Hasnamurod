MISSION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy aims to produce enlightened, competitive, and well-balanced graduates committed to promoting Islamic culture and becoming proactive members of society through acquisition of holistic knowledge, appropriate skills, and worthy attitudes and values.

VISION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy envisions to be a leading Muslim private educational institution in the delivery of quality dunya-akhirat education and a potential partner in nation-building.

GOALS

1. To role model an Islamically-oriented school administration and management;

2. To provide quality dunya-akhirat education from elementary to high school levels;

3. To develop the pupils and students upholding Tawhidic principles and culture of Islam;

4. To develop and sustain capabilities of school administration, teaching, and non-teaching personnel;

5. To deliver relevant educational programs and services to all pupils, students, parents, and other stakeholders;

6. To establish and strengthen linkages and networks with government agencies and non-government organizations and partners for academic and infrastructure development;

7. To foster unity and cooperation among other schools and institutions for the advancement of Bangsamoro education.
