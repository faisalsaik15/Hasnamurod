MISSION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy aims to produce enlightened, competitive, and well-balanced graduates committed to promoting Islamic culture and being proactive members of society through holistic knowledge, appropriate skills, and strong values.

VISION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy envisions becoming a leading Muslim private educational institution delivering quality Dunya-Akhirat education and serving as a potential partner in nation-building.

GOALS

1. To serve as a role model for Islamically-oriented school administration and management.
2. To provide quality Dunya-Akhirat education from elementary to high school levels.
3. To instill Tawhidic principles and Islamic values in students.
4. To develop and sustain the capabilities of school administrators, teachers, and staff.
5. To deliver relevant educational programs and services to all stakeholders.
6. To strengthen partnerships with government and non-government organizations for academic and infrastructure development.
7. To foster unity and cooperation among schools and institutions for the advancement of Bangsamoro education.
