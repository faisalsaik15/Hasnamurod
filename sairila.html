MISSION

Ibn Taimyah Foundation Academy aims into produce enlightened,competitive and well balance graduates committed to promote Islamic culture and become proactive member of the society through acquisition of hollistic knowledge,appropriate skills,and worthy attitude and values.

VISION

Ibn Taimyah Foundation Academy envision to be a leading Muslim private educational institution in the delivery of quality dunya-akhirat education and potentional partner in nation building.

GOALS

1.To role model an islamically-oriented school administration and management;
2.To provide quality dunya-akhirat education from elementary to highschool levels;
3.To develop the pupils and student uploading tawhidic principles and culture to islam;
4.To develop and sustain capabilities of school administrators,teaching and nonteaching personel;
5.To deliver relevant educational programs and services to all pupils,student,and other stakeholders;
6.To establish and strengthen linkages and network with goverment agencies and non-goverment organizations and partner for academic and infastracture development;
7.To foster unity and cooperation among other school and institutions for advancement of Bangsamoro education.
